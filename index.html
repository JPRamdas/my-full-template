The Joy of Foraging-Wellbeing & Free Food

Community

In the Valley we are all about the community, we come together to share, help each other out and of course looking after the environment while enjoying what it can offer for free.

Fun For Kids

We encorage kids involvement with all our activities and events. We want them to built a good knowledge about food availability, the resources around them. Enjoying and respecting the natural world and it is always best to start at home.

Health

Being outdoor bring a lot of benefits for our Health. Foraging in most cases are gentle and slow walk through nature. The double benefit of course is that you get to cook and eat something healthy and organic provided by nature.
